About YYC Cards & Collectibles Show

A Calgary-built show for collectors, families, and vendors who love cards, collectibles, and the thrill of the hunt.

Our Mission What You’ll Find Our Values

Our Mission

Create the best collector experience in Calgary—simple, fun, and welcoming.

Collector-Focused

Bring together the hobby’s best vendors and the inventory you actually want to see.

Family Friendly

Wide aisles, clear signage, and activities that make the day easy for all ages.

Local Energy

Support Calgary shops, sellers, and collectors while growing the community.

What You’ll Find

A curated mix of sports, TCG, and pop culture collectibles.

Cards & TCG

  • Hockey, NBA, NFL, MLB
  • Pokémon, Yu-Gi-Oh, MTG
  • Singles, sealed, and sets

Graded & Premium

  • PSA, SGC, BGS slabs
  • High-end modern and vintage
  • Display cases and showcases

Collectibles

  • Toys, comics, and nostalgia
  • Pop culture and retro items
  • Supplies and accessories

Show Values
