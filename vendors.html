What Vendors Can Sell

We focus on cards, collectibles, and hobby-related inventory.

Approved Categories

  • Sports cards, Pokémon, and TCG
  • Graded slabs, sealed product, singles
  • Collectibles, toys, comics, nostalgia

Not Allowed

  • Weapons or prohibited items
  • Unlicensed merchandise
  • Non-hobby retail products
