Common Questions

Quick answers for vendors and sponsors.

Vendor FAQs

When do vendors load in?

Vendor load-in runs from 7:00–9:00am on show day. We share dock access details after confirmation.

Can I request tables together?

Yes. Include a note with the names of vendors you want to be near and we’ll do our best.

Do you provide chairs?

Every table includes at least one chair. Multi-table bookings include additional seating.

Sponsor FAQs

What sponsor assets are required?

We typically request logo files, brand colors, and a short blurb. We’ll share a checklist after you inquire.

Can I customize a sponsor package?

Absolutely. We can tailor signage, on-site activations, and digital placements.

Do sponsors get vendor tables?

Event Sponsor and Title Sponsor packages include a vendor table. We can add tables to other tiers.
