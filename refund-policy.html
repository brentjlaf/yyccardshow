Clear policies for tickets and vendor tables.

Ticket Refunds

If your plans change, let us know as soon as possible.

  • Tickets are refundable up to 7 days before the event.
  • Transfers to another attendee are allowed anytime.
  • Eventbrite/Square fees may be non-refundable.

Vendor Table Refunds

Vendor tables are limited and managed carefully.

  • Full refunds available up to 14 days before the event.
  • Partial refunds may be available within 7 days at our discretion.
  • No refunds for no-shows on event day.
