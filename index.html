What to Bring (Get Better Deals)

Small things that make your day smoother and your buying smarter.

Cash + Card

Many vendors take card, but cash moves faster and often gets better pricing.

Tip: small bills help in bargain bins.

Supplies

Bring sleeves/toploaders for big hits and a bag/box to protect purchases.

Optional: small flashlight for surface checks.

Trade Prep

If you’re trading, have comps saved on your phone and your cards organized.

Keep it fast: step aside to negotiate.
