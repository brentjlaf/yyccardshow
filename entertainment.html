Experience Boosters

Entertainment That Helps Sales

Keep entertainment on the perimeter so it adds energy without stealing attention from vendors.

Best Options Optional Avoid These

✅ Best Add-Ons (Low Risk, High ROI)

Keep it simple, quiet, and revenue-friendly.

Raffles / Giveaways

  • Entry tickets or cards
  • Use sponsor prizes
  • Run at exit for last-minute sales

Face Painting

  • Family-friendly
  • Quiet activity
  • Works near back wall

Card Artist / Sketches

  • Creates Instagram content
  • Premium feel
  • Minimal crowding risk

⚠️ Conditional / Optional

Only if controlled and placed off the main aisles.

Low-Volume Live Breaks

  • Approve 1–2 breakers
  • No yelling, no aisle crowding
  • Set rules in advance

Mascot / Costume

  • Scheduled photo times only
  • Great for families
  • Keep near photo spot

Mini TCG Tournament

  • Pre-register only
  • Short time slots
  • Don’t use prime floor space

❌ Avoid These (They Hurt Sales)

These create bottlenecks or take buyers away from tables.

  • Loud DJs or music
  • Arcade machines / consoles
  • Bounce houses
  • Celebrity guests for show #1

Why They Fail

They increase noise, reduce browsing, and create crowding in the aisles.

Ideal Entertainment Mix (First Show)

Keep it light and focused.

Core

  • Raffles (yes)
  • Face painting
  • One card artist

Optional

  • One controlled breaker
  • Trivia or trade challenge

Placement Rule

  • Entertainment on the perimeter
  • Never block aisles
  • Let vendors be the main show
