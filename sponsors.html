Sponsorship Tiers

Choose the level that fits your goals.

Title Sponsor

$1,000
  • Naming: “YYC Cards & Collectibles Show presented by [Sponsor]” on website + key graphics
  • Top placement: largest logo on homepage, tickets page, and sponsor page
  • On-site visibility: banner/signage at entrance + stage/announce area + sponsor wall
  • Premium booth: best table location (near entrance or main aisle) + 1 vendor table included
  • Mentions: stage mentions during raffles + 3 social posts + 6 story mentions
  • Raffle integration: sponsor-branded raffle/giveaway mention (you provide prize or we co-fund)
  • Email list: logo + link in attendee email/update (if you send updates)
  • Post-show: featured in recap post + tagged in photo/video highlights

Gold Sponsor

$500
  • Strong placement: logo on website + posters/flyers + sponsor wall (medium size)
  • Vendor table included: 1 table + chair (standard placement)
  • Social: 2 feed posts + 4 story mentions + tag on sponsor announcement
  • On-site: signage at your table + mention during at least 1 raffle draw
  • Promo option: coupons/business cards at the door or info table (you supply)
  • Community perk: ability to sponsor a “Kids Free” or “Trade Zone” sign (branding on that area)
  • Post-show: included in recap + sponsor thank-you graphic

Community Sponsor

$200
  • Logo placement: sponsor wall + sponsor page listing (small logo)
  • Shoutouts: 1 on-site announcement + mention on sponsor thank-you post
  • Social spotlight: 1 story feature (tag + short blurb about what you do)
  • Support a feature: branding on one small area sign (Trade Zone / Kids Corner / Raffle Table)
  • Promo option: small stack of flyers/cards at info table (you supply)
