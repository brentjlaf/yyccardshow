Terms and Conditions

By purchasing a ticket, attending the event, applying as a vendor, sponsoring, or otherwise participating, you agree to the terms below.

Questions? Contact Us Code of Conduct

1) Definitions

Clear terms used on this page.

  • Organizer / Event: YYC Cards & Collectibles Show and its staff/representatives.
  • Venue: the facility hosting the event, including parking lots and common areas.
  • Attendee: any person entering the venue during event hours.
  • Vendor: any person/business renting a table/booth space or selling/displaying goods.
  • Sponsor: any person/business providing money, goods, or services for promotional placement.

2) Admission + Attendance Rules

Respect the venue, staff, and other attendees.

  • All attendees must follow posted venue rules and staff instructions.
  • The Organizer may refuse entry or remove any person for safety, policy violations, disorderly conduct, or suspected unlawful activity.
  • Removal for cause may result in no refund.
  • Re-entry may require a wristband, stamp, or other proof of admission.
  • Event hours, programming, and vendor list may change without notice.
  • Minors must be supervised by a parent/guardian at all times.

3) Tickets + Payments

How tickets are issued and used.

  • Tickets are valid only for the date and event listed on the ticket.
  • Tickets may be sold online and/or at the door depending on capacity.
  • Online ticket fees (if any) are set by the ticket platform and may be non-refundable.
  • Lost tickets/wristbands may not be replaceable without proof of purchase.
  • Ticket resales are at your own risk; the Organizer is not responsible for fraudulent resale transactions.
  • All prices are subject to applicable taxes where required.

4) Health + Safety

We prioritize a safe, family-friendly environment.

  • Do not run, shove, or block aisles, exits, or vendor tables.
  • Follow capacity limits, queue lines, and staff direction at all times.
  • No weapons, illegal substances, or dangerous items are permitted.
  • Service animals are welcome where permitted by law and venue policy.
  • The Organizer may request that oversized bags/cases be repositioned or removed if they create safety issues.

5) Trades, Purchases + Disputes

Buying and trading is at your own discretion.

  • All purchases and trades are private transactions between buyers and sellers.
  • The Organizer does not guarantee authenticity, grading accuracy, condition, or value of any item sold or traded.
  • Inspect items before purchasing/trading. Ask questions and confirm terms before exchanging money/items.
  • Chargebacks, refunds, or disputes related to vendor sales must be handled directly with that vendor.
  • Use designated trade areas where available and do not trade in aisles or block vendor traffic.

6) Prohibited Items + Counterfeit Policy

Protecting attendees and the hobby.

  • Counterfeit, bootleg, stolen, or illegal items are strictly prohibited.
  • The Organizer may remove prohibited items, require a vendor to stop selling specific items, or remove a vendor without refund for serious violations.
  • If you suspect counterfeit or stolen goods, report it to staff. Do not confront others aggressively.
  • The Organizer may cooperate with venue security or law enforcement where necessary.

7) Photography, Video + Media

How media is handled at the event.

  • The Organizer may photograph or record video/audio for promotional or documentation purposes.
  • By attending, you consent to being photographed/recorded in public event areas (unless prohibited by law).
  • Personal photography is generally allowed, but do not disrupt vendors or block aisles.
  • No filming of vendor inventory “close-ups” for commercial purposes without vendor permission.
  • Press/media requests should be directed to the Organizer via the Contact page.

8) Vendor Terms (High-Level)

A summary — full details are in the Vendor Agreement.

  • Vendors must follow setup/teardown times, table assignment instructions, and venue rules.
  • Vendors are responsible for their own merchandise, displays, cash handling, and payment systems.
  • Vendors must keep their area safe: no unstable shelving, exposed hazards, or aisle obstructions.
  • No counterfeit items. No unlawful items. No harassment of attendees or other vendors.
  • The Organizer may relocate tables for safety/flow, or to comply with venue requirements.
  • Vendors must not operate loud audio, disruptive streaming, or activities that interfere with neighboring tables.
  • Vendor table refunds/transfer rules are governed by the Vendor Agreement.
