FAQ

Quick answers for first-time visitors.

Is it just cards?

No—expect collectibles, toys, comics, and pop culture items too.

Can I trade?

Yes. Trading is welcome, and most vendors are happy to work with collectors.

Do vendors take card?

Most do, but cash is always the fastest for deals.

Is there food?

Snacks and nearby options are available, plus easy re-entry.
